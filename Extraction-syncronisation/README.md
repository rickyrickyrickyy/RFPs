# 2.1b Extraction & synchronisation

**RFP section:** Part of intake (2.1). Driven peelers, driven bracket (yoke mounts), fruit support, roller, chute deflectors. Stages oranges from the feeder and meshes with static peelers for extraction.

## Scope

- Fruit synchronisation — roller pushes open feeder spring to drop one orange onto fruit support; oranges staged to drop into peelers’ jaws
- Driven peelers (mesh with static peelers in Collection)
- Driven bracket — yoke mount holes on back; carries load, mashes against static peelers
- Chute deflectors (red) — prevent peel from falling into the chutes during the compression stroke of the juicing cycle
- **Fruit support** — 3 pairs of flat angled rails (height tallest at ends, taper to center); connecting link; indexing slots; base bolted to drive bracket; rails angled for CIP spray-through; 3°→8° angle (5° slope on driven bracket); pusher (dark green) for roller attachment (cut/welded sheet, candidate for formed component); central pin, roller

See also **[Feeder](../Feeder/)** for full intake feeder system (2.1).
