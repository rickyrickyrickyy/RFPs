P6 RFP — Delijuice 870/970

Industrial prototype subsystem RFPs. Drawings target: April 1st (Sean/YES). JX operations: October.

Introduction

P6 is the current industrial prototype (Delijuice 870/970). Jiangxi (JX) factory with LTA; miniplant 8,160 L/h, 6 extractors. Each subsystem section includes a gallery of figures and, where filled in, component lists, interfaces, and design notes. Refer to Project overview for the full subsystem list and context.

CAD colours: Colour keys are per subsystem. Each section has its own legend for the figures in that section. Use the legend on the relevant subsystem page when reading its CAD views.
