Collection subsystem

Static peelers (#F36F3B), static deflectors (#CCDF3F), juice collectors (Y-tubes), filter tube, plug cutter, collection mount plate. Mounts on the frame’s loaded mount plate.

Reminder: Send the 34 mm standard connector for quick connection (juice collector Y-tube outlets).

Function

  • Driven and static peelers (#F36F3B) mesh; oranges fall between them; fingers interlace → fruit mashed, peel squeezed into extraction chamber and outflow chute. Static deflectors (#CCDF3F) keep peels sliding towards the static plate (see Figure 1, Figure 5, Figure 10).
  • Plug cutter (#4DB7F5) cuts a cylindrical plug from the centre of oranges during juicing.
  • Juice flows out of oranges through slots in the filter tube (#287F3F) into the yellow collector (#FFFF50), then out via the Y-branched tube to a ribbed connection for standard rubber pipes.

Extraction cycle and disposal

After the pressing stroke (juice to Y-section): the plug ejection system pushes a plunger through the filter, pushes the plug back out of the plug cutter, and the plug falls into the core chute of the disposal system. Meanwhile, peels fall to the sides of the peelers, avoid the core chutes, and separate into two channels; the disposal system (two augers) takes peels and plugs/cores away separately.

Interfaces

  • Mount: Collection mount plate (#27C16A) on loaded mount plate (frame).
  • Input: Fruit from extraction/synchronisation; driven peelers mesh with static peelers.
  • Output: Peel → extraction chamber / outflow chute; juice → filter → yellow collector → Y-tube → ribbed connection (34 mm standard connector for quick connection).
