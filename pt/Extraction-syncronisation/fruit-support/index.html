Suporte de frutas (subassembly)

Subassembly of 4. Extração e sincronização. Directs fruit from the pipe to the peeler; oranges stage here then drop into the descascadores’ jaws.

Legenda de cores e componentes

Applies to the CAD views in this section. In the main extraction assembly this subassembly appears as one colour ( bright green); detail views use the colours below.

Cor(es)Componente
Base plate — bolted to drive bracket
Rails — outer (tallest), middle, inner (shortest) pairs; height tapers to center so fruit rolls to center
Connecting link — spacing and parallelism between rails
Pusher and pusher support — roller attached with bolt

Propósito & design

Directs fruit from the pipe to the peeler and reduces risk of fruit falling to the sides.

  • 3 pairs of flat angled rails. Height tallest at the ends, tapers to lowest at the center so oranges roll to the center two rails. Length varies to match tube diameter.
  • Connecting link maintains spacing and parallelism between rails and perpendicularity to the plate. Indexing slots for inserting rails.
  • Top edge of rails filleted to prevent cutting fruit. Base bolted to drive bracket for easy removal (e.g. for replacing rollers).
  • CIP: Angled rails allow clean-in-place spray through gaps to the descascadores below.
  • Rail angle: Downwards so oranges roll forward. Current 3°; target 8° (5° slope added to suporte acionado). From front, rails higher on sides than center.
  • Pusher: Designed as cut/welded sheet; could be a formed component.

Improvement targets: 8° rail angle, formed pusher. Manufacturing note: the suporte de frutas can be manufactured in any practical way as long as the rail geometry, 3–8° slope/bowl shape, and cleanability (no pockets, smooth drainage) are met. Forming is a candidate method, but the exact formed-part design is left to the contractor/ODM to propose.

Interfaces

  • Mount: Base bolted to suporte acionado (extraction/sync).
  • Input: Fruit from feeder (drops onto suporte de frutas).
  • Output: Fruit rolls to center rails and drops into descascadores’ jaws.
  • Pusher/roller: Roller (extraction/sync) attached with bolt; interfaces with feeder sync spring.
