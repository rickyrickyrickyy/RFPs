Suporte de acionamento do êmbolo

Actuation bracket that pushes/pulls the plunger bases only in the travel direction; designed to avoid racking moments and keep plungers concentric with the filter tube.

Introdução

The suporte de acionamento do êmbolo couples the drivetrain actuation (via plunger drive rods) to the four core ejection plungers. Its key requirement is kinematic purity: the bracket must apply only axial force (forward/back along the machine’s ejection axis) and must not introduce left/right/up/down moments to the plungers. Maintaining this avoids the critical failure mode of plunger racking and sticking.

Legenda de cores e componentes

Drive bracket, actuation interfaces, and (optional) guillotine-style insertion/removal mechanism.

Cor(es)Componente
—Suporte de acionamento do êmbolo — carries four plungers and moves in the ejection travel direction.
—Guillotine / sliding insertion access — optional mechanism that allows the plungers to be removed for service while keeping the push/pull behavior constrained.

Discussão

Componentees

  • Drive bracket — supports the plunger bases; constrained so it moves forward/back only in the ejection axis.
  • Service access constraint — ensure the insertion/removal mechanism does not compromise axial alignment during normal operation.

Interfaces

  • Input: plunger drive rods actuated from the drivetrain/transmissão yoke system.
  • Output: plungers push into filter tube and retract out of the tube to eject cores.
  • Mount: guided by the side-panel linear bearing housings to ensure parallel rod/plunger motion.

Interfaces e tolerâncias

The bracket must keep plungers concentric with filter tubes/plug cutter to prevent racking and ensure sealing performance.

Problemas e riscos conhecidos

  • Racking risk — if any moment is introduced, plungers can rack in the filter tube, leading to sticking and damage.
  • Sticking/jam failure mode — a stuck plunger can propagate forces into the drive mechanism; bracket must be designed to minimize moment amplification.

DFM e fabricação (China)

  • Optional guillotine approach — sliding rails + central plate “door” to allow plungers to be removed for service while preserving the constrained push/pull engagement during operation.
  • Low-friction materials — consider engineering plastics (e.g., Delrin / fiber-reinforced polymer) where sliding interfaces are required, but ensure any food-zone contact remains safe.

Perguntas para o contratado

  1. Propose an insertion/removal mechanism for plungers (guillotine vs alternate) that preserves axial-only actuation and repeatable concentricity.
  2. Define how to validate that the bracket applies no meaningful moments to plungers (tests/inspection/measurement approach).
  3. Propose materials for sliding interfaces that are low friction yet compatible with washdown and food safety (including microplastic risk).
