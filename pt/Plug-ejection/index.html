Discussão

Ejeção de caroço (cycle)

  1. Pressing stroke: Juice to collector Y-section; core remains in plug cutter / filter.
  2. Ejeção de caroço: Plunger pushes through filter, ejects core from plug cutter → core falls into core chute (disposal).
  3. Descarte: Peels to sides of descascadores; two augers take peels and cores away separately.

Interfaces

  • Input: Core in plug cutter/filter after pressing stroke (from Coleta).
  • Output: Core ejected → falls into disposal core chute.
  • Drive: Plunger actuation from drivetrain / transmissão (yoke/cam).
