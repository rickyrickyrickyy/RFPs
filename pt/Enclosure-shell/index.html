9. Enclosure / carcaça

Subpáginas

  • Coberturas protetoras (êmbolo, acionamento) (com conteúdo) — CAD figure from shared enclosure gallery.

Introdução

Protective covers over moving parts. Relacionado to Estrutura. The feeder, drivetrain lid, and plunger lid are mounted using overcentered hinges.

Legenda de cores e componentes

Two main covers + overcentered hinges. Alimentador, drivetrain lid, and plunger lid all use overcentered hinges (open stay open, shut stay shut).

Cor(es)Componente
Plunger cover — protects core ejection plungers
Acionamento cover — covers drivetrain
Overcentered hinge — linkage/bracket; mounting to covers and frame
Base / frame — structural support for covers
Hinge design (WIP): Springs and assembly method for overcentered hinges not yet designed.
