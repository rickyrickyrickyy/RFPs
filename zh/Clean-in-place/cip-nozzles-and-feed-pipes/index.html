CIP nozzles and feed pipes

部件 of the 就地清洗（CIP） section. Nozzle layout + rigid stainless manifolds + flexible hose interfaces to the plant CIP line.

简介

This page captures the spray nozzles that clean the interior of P6 and the piping strategy connecting them to the plant CIP system. The feeder and peel-deflector nozzle groups each connect through rigid stainless pipes to a single input connection, with flexible hoses to allow opening/closing and service motion.

讨论

Nozzle locations (per P6 concept)

Locations A–E correspond to your coworker’s CIP spec. Exact nozzle models, spray angles, and flow rates are TBD and must be validated by cleaning tests.

  • A (feeder curve, circular jet) — one nozzle per feeding tube, aimed into the curved section (tube turned backwards).
  • B (behind feeding tube, angled jet) — one nozzle per peeler aimed at the movable peeler around mid-stroke.
  • C (front of feeding tube, angled jet) — one nozzle per peeler aimed at the fixed peeler.
  • D (rear plate of peel deflector, angled jet) — one nozzle per peeler aimed at the movable peeler (mid-stroke); mounted below 削皮器. Spray reach must clean surfaces behind/around the driven chute deflector that covers core chutes during compression.
  • E (front plate of peel collector, angled jet) — one nozzle per peeler plus one additional nozzle (total 5) positioned between core ducts and at both sides, avoiding deflector hardware (updated concept: 导流板 are primarily driven/under-peeler).

Nozzle counts

  • Total: 21 spray nozzles
  • 进料器 lid: 12 (4 stations × 3 nozzles per station: A + B + C)
  • Below movable 削皮器: 4 (4 stations × 1 nozzle: D)
  • Below static 削皮器: 5 (E: one per peeler +1)

Piping / manifold strategy

  • Rigid stainless pipes connect nozzles into manifolds for each group (feeder lid group, peel deflector group).
  • Flexible hose connects each manifold group to the plant CIP line, allowing the feeder to open/close while connected.
  • Single input per group is preferred for integration and fast hookup.

承包商问题

  1. Select nozzle type(s) and spray patterns for each location (A–E) to achieve full coverage with minimal overspray and no collision.
  2. Specify pipe sizes, fitting standards, and flexible hose spec (pressure rating, bend radius, sanitary requirements) for both manifold groups.
  3. Define operating targets to validate in first tests: pressure, flow rate, temperature, concentration, and cycle durations (plant-controlled).

接口

  • Input: plant CIP supply via flexible hose to each manifold group.
  • Output: spray coverage over feeding tubes, 削皮器, 导流板, and collection region as defined by nozzle map A–E.
  • Mount: nozzles mount to feeder lid assemblies and peel-deflector/collector structures; routing must allow access and feeder motion.
