讨论

初步设计与意图

  • System goal — Release one orange per cycle into each extraction station.
  • Flow sequence — Upstream sorter/lines → adjustable 进料管 → 90° tube → orange rests on sync spring while a column stacks above → pusher opens spring during compression to drop one orange onto 水果托架 → spring closes on return stroke to hold the next orange while the staged orange drops into the 削皮器.
  • Modularity — 进料子组件 tubes are riveted to a mount plate and the mount plate is riveted to the 进料器本体 so modules can be swapped/iterated by drilling out rivets.

已知问题与风险

  • Jams / double-drop — Sync spring must reliably gate a fruit column and release only one orange at a time without wedging.
  • Collision envelope — 水果托架 and pusher must never collide with 进料管/90° tubes; pusher lateral flex can cause catastrophic collisions with tubes/lids.
  • Washdown — This region is in/near splash zone; edges and joints must be cleanable and safe to handle.

DFM 与制造（中国）

  • Tube standards — 进料子组件 uses 3″ NPS / DN90 Sch 5S as a baseline; contractor may choose China-available equivalents that preserve function and cleanability.
  • Hinge/pins — Entry tube adjustability relies on pinned joints; contractor to propose robust hardware and anti-loosening.
  • CIP nozzle integration — 90° tube includes a port/tube for a nozzle to spray down the entry/spring zone; nozzle and mounting details TBD.

承包商问题

  1. Validate the sync spring gating concept for reliable one-at-a-time release (fruit size range, friction, jam/double-drop failure modes) and propose improvements.
  2. Propose tube/hinge/pin designs that are adjustable, rigid when locked, and fast to disassemble for seasonal maintenance.
  3. Propose a CIP nozzle placement and cleaning strategy for the entry tube and spring region (spray direction, drain paths, avoiding dead zones).

子组件

  • 进料子组件 — mount plate, flanged 90° entry tube, straight tube and spring guide, sync spring (8 CAD views, colour key, parameters).
  • 进料器本体, 90° entry tube, external duct — detail pages TBD.

接口

  • 进料器 hinge — 进料器本体 rotates for access and CIP; overcentered hinge (see 外壳/外壳).
  • Brace — welded to lid; supports 进料子组件 and 进料管.
  • Fruit flow — 进料管 → 进料子组件 → extraction & synchronisation (水果托架, 削皮器).
