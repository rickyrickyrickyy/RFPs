导流板

Required core-chute coverage during compression: deflector hardware on the moving driven peeler underside (static 导流板 removed).

简介

The disposal flow requires that peels do not drop directly into the core chute stream during compression. In the latest concept, static 导流板 on the collection mount plate are removed; instead, a deflector under the driven 削皮器 covers the core chutes during the compression stroke and ensures peel is routed to the peel chute.

颜色说明与部件

Deflector hardware is part of the moving extraction bracket peeler stack; disposal page documents the interface and requirements.

颜色部件
—Driven underside deflector — stiff formed/bent plate(s) mounted under driven peeler modules to cover core chutes during compression.
—Core-chute interface — clearance envelope vs. core tubes and peel flow paths.

讨论

部件

  • Formed/bent deflector plate(s) mounted under driven 削皮器 to cover the core chute openings during compression.
  • Mounting interface to the 从动支架 and peeler modules; must be collision-free and food-safe/cleanable.

接口

  • Input: peel trajectory and compression-stroke position from extraction & synchronisation.
  • Output: peel routing away from core chute stream into peel chute.
  • Mount: driven peeler underside / 从动支架 interface (moving component).

接口与公差

The deflector must maintain clearance and coverage over the full compression stroke without collision. CIP spray must be able to reach around/through the deflector as designed.

已知问题与风险

  • Deflector flex — if deflector bends under vibration/juice impacts, peel may enter core chute and/or surfaces may collide with moving parts.
  • CIP blind zones — deflector can block spray; must verify spray pattern can clean both front and underside surfaces.

承包商问题

  1. Propose a stiff, manufacturable deflector design (formed/bent) that maintains clearance during motion.
  2. Specify mounting method and fastening strategy suitable for washdown and food safety (cleanable, no crevices/pockets).
  3. Propose a deflector + CIP spray compatibility test plan (spray reach validation + cleaning effectiveness verification).
