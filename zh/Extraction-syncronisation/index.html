简介

Stages oranges from the feeder and meshes with static 削皮器 for extraction. Yokes from 传动 drive the bracket and 削皮器.

Peel 导流板: The chute deflector (this subsystem) under the driven peeler modules is the deflector required to cover the core chutes during compression and route peel correctly. Static 导流板 on the collection mount plate are removed (figures/text refresh pending).

讨论

初步设计与意图

  • What this subsystem does — Receive single oranges from the feeder and stage them on the 水果托架, then drive the extraction event by meshing driven 削皮器 with static 削皮器 (collection). The 传动 yoke/shafts push this module into the collection module during compression.
  • Design status — Peeler core geometry is inherited from a commercial machine. The mounting flanges for both driven and static 削皮器 (and how they are manufactured) are not finalized and are planned as machined-on flanges added to existing 削皮器.
  • Hygiene/washdown — This is inside the splash zone. All surfaces should be spray-down washable with no pockets/shelves; top faces should be sloped for drainage. Fasteners should follow hygienic practice (e.g., avoid Phillips/slotted where possible; prefer hex heads).

已知问题与风险

  • High force path — The 从动支架 carries large loads and bending moments: 传动 shafts push on a short bracket while supporting four peeler modules. Stiffness and fatigue life are critical.
  • Peeler alignment — Driven 削皮器 must be concentric/aligned with static 削皮器. Current plan relies on an adjust-then-lock approach (loose fit holes for adjustment, then drill/ream and add shoulder bolts/dowels).
  • Synchronization pusher stiffness — Only one pusher is used across four modules, pushing a diagonal spring. If the pusher or 水果托架 flexes laterally (X-axis), it can collide with tubes/lids and cause catastrophic damage.
  • 导流板 are WIP — Chute 导流板 must prevent peel from falling into core chutes during compression and redirect peel into peel chute; current concept is not yet constrained/stiff.

DFM 与制造（中国）

  • 从动支架 — Current intent is a sand-cast 从动支架 with draft angles and post-machining for critical mounting faces/holes. Contractor to confirm rib thickness, casting quality, and whether cast vs welded is best.
  • Surface finishing — Consider post-processing for the cast bracket to improve cleanability (e.g., smoothing/flow finishing) so there are no crevices where pulp can accumulate.
  • Peeler flange fabrication — Plan is to machine flanges (lathe) and weld a plate to existing 削皮器 for bolt-up. Contractor to propose a robust welding + inspection approach that preserves geometry and hygiene.
  • 水果托架 — Current design uses waterjet-cut ~5 mm plates assembled with slots and welded. Stiffness must prevent sag and lateral flex, while allowing CIP spray through rails/gaps.

寻求的优化

  • Alignment repeatability — Fast alignment procedure that can be repeated seasonally (adjust → lock → dowel/shoulder-bolt).
  • Cleanability — No pockets; sloped top faces; rail gaps that allow spray-through cleaning of 削皮器.
  • Stiffness — Increase stiffness of 从动支架 and 水果托架 to prevent collisions and preserve peeler alignment.

承包商问题

  1. Propose the best manufacturing strategy for the 从动支架 (cast vs welded) given washdown/hygiene requirements, stiffness needs, and China supply chain.
  2. Propose a peeler alignment procedure and tolerances for concentricity/mesh so 削皮器 do not collide and wear is controlled.
  3. Review the 水果托架 + pusher stiffness requirements and propose design changes that prevent lateral flex and collisions while keeping CIP spray-through.
  4. Propose a chute deflector design that is stiff, manufacturable (formed/bent), and does not collide during motion; ensure peel is routed to peel chute (not core chute).
