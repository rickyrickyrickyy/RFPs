简介

Protective covers over moving parts. 相关 to 机架. The feeder, drivetrain lid, and plunger lid are mounted using overcentered hinges.

颜色说明与部件

Two main covers + overcentered hinges. 进料器, drivetrain lid, and plunger lid all use overcentered hinges (open stay open, shut stay shut).

颜色部件
Plunger cover — protects core ejection plungers
传动系统 cover — covers drivetrain
Overcentered hinge — linkage/bracket; mounting to covers and frame
Base / frame — structural support for covers
Hinge design (WIP): Springs and assembly method for overcentered hinges not yet designed.
