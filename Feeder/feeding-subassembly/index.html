Feeding subassembly

Part of the Fruit intake system. Feeds oranges one at a time to the extraction/synchronisation mechanism.

Description

The feeding subassembly regulates the flow of oranges from the entry tubes and releases them one at a time onto the fruit support. Rollers on the extraction/synchronisation system push open the yellow sync spring to allow a single orange to drop. The whole subassembly is mounted to a tan mount plate , which is then mounted onto the feeder body.

Assembly structure

  • Mount plate — tan; base of subassembly; mounts to feeder body.
  • Entry tube assembly (welded unit, riveted to mount plate):
    • Flanged 90° entry tube — green; curved tube where oranges roll down to the feed.
    • Entry tube flange — pink.
    • Entry tube mount plate — light green.
  • Flanged straight tube and spring guide (welded unit, riveted to mount plate):
    • Flange — beige.
    • Straight tube — purple.
    • Spring guide — dark blue/teal.
  • Sync spring mechanism — yellow sync spring; pushed open by rollers on the extraction/sync system to drop an orange onto the fruit support. The sync spring is mounted to 2× dark green sync spring mounts on two pink rollers.

Spring sizing design parameters

  • Spring placed at 45° angle to tube (110 mm forward of tube center, 250 mm behind).
  • Fruit support pusher is placed 5 mm away from tube at closest point to prevent collision.
  • Pusher extends the spring 5 mm past the outside of the tube.
  • Pusher range 178 mm as determined by 870 drivetrain.

Spring specification

  • Static length = 250 mm
  • Wire diameter = 2 mm
  • Outer diameter = 22 mm
  • In the static position, the spring is 250 mm in length; in the stretched position, 306 mm.

90° pipe parameters

Refers to the flanged 90° entry tube .

  • Standard: 3″ NPS / 90 DN Pipe, Schedule 5S — manufacturers should choose whatever is available to them in China with similar specs.
  • Food-zone flow constraint: target inner diameter is ~90 mm (choose the closest China-available pipe meeting the internal flow diameter requirement).
  • Dimensions: 88.9 mm OD, 2.11 mm wall thickness.
  • Slotted index for welded flanges.
  • Curved tube cut at 62°.
  • Vertical cut-out for CIP spray nozzle — included in the latest CAD; photos/figures should be refreshed to show the final nozzle position.

Interfaces

  • Input: Oranges from beige entry tubes (feeder top-level) roll into the 90° entry tube.
  • Output: Rollers on the extraction/synchronisation system push open the yellow sync spring; one orange drops onto the fruit support.
  • Mount: Subassembly mount plate attaches to the feeder body.

Fruit placement guardrail (left/right tube alignment)

  • Verify that the left/right tube positions release fruit directly onto the correct rails/landing zones on the fruit support.
  • Treat tube lateral placement as a functional tolerance because misplacement increases jam/collision risk.
