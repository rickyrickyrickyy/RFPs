2.2 Plug ejection / plunger drive / juice collection

Plunger, filter, plug cutter interface; plug ejection into disposal core chute.

Plug ejection (cycle)

  1. Pressing stroke: Juice to collector Y-section; plug remains in plug cutter / filter.
  2. Plug ejection: Plunger pushes through filter, ejects plug from plug cutter → plug falls into core chute (disposal).
  3. Disposal: Peels to sides of peelers; two augers take peels and plugs/cores away separately.

Colour key & components

Plunger assembly, drive bracket, juice outflow — detail pages TBD. Green housings enclose spring and guide rods; interface with filter/plug-cutter and core chute.
