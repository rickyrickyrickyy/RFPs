# 2.2 Plug ejection / plunger drive / juice collection

**RFP section:** Plunger drive and juice collection, including **plug ejection**. Add photos, Q&A, and part/parameter details.

## Plug ejection (extraction cycle)

After the **pressing stroke** of the extraction cycle (juice has left the oranges into the collector Y-section), the **plug ejection system** pushes a **plunger** through the **filter**, pushes the **plug** back out of the **plug cutter**, and the plug **falls into the core chute** of the [disposal system](../Outflow-disposal/). Meanwhile, peels fall to the sides of the peelers, avoid the core chutes, and separate into two channels; the disposal augers take peels and plugs/cores away separately.

## Components

- **Juice outflow collection** — filter, collector Y-tubes (see [Collection](../Collection/))
- **Filter** — juice through slots; plunger passes through for plug ejection
- **Plunger** — ejects plug through filter out of plug cutter
- **Plunger drive bracket**
- **Plug cutter** — (in Collection); plug cut during juicing, ejected by this system
