# Outflow / disposal — images

Disposal system: peel chute, core chutes (welded to peel chute), two augers. Separates and conveys peels and plugs/cores for separate processing.

## CAD colour key (main)

| Colour | Hex | Component |
|--------|-----|-----------|
| Red | #D43C3C | **Peel chute** — folded sheet metal; peels fall to sides of peelers, avoid core chutes, two channels |
| (same red) | #D43C3C | **Core chutes** — welded to peel chute; plugs/cores fall here after plug ejection |
| Teal | #28B399 | **Two augers** — take peels and plugs/cores away separately |

## Alternative colour scheme

| Colour | Hex | Component |
|--------|-----|-----------|
| Blue | #2768BC | Peel chute |
| Orange-brown | #D79132 | Core chutes |
| Light yellow | #FFED9E | Two augers |

## Function (brief)

After the pressing stroke: **plug ejection** pushes plunger through filter, plug exits plug cutter and **falls into core chute**. **Peels** fall to the sides of the peelers, avoid the core chutes, and **separate into two channels** for separate processing. The **two augers** take peels and plugs/cores away separately.
