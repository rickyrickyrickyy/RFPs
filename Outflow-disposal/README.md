# 2.4 Outflow / disposal subsystem

**Peel chute** (#D43C3C, folded sheet metal), **core chutes** (welded to peel chute), **two augers** (#28B399). Take peels and plugs/cores away separately.

## Function (cycle context)

- After the **pressing stroke**, juice goes to the collector Y-section ([Collection](../Collection/)).
- **Plug ejection** ([Plug-ejection](../Plug-ejection/)): plunger through filter → plug out of plug cutter → **plug falls into core chute**.
- **Peels** fall to the **sides of the peelers**, **avoid the core chutes**, **two channels** for separate processing.
- **Two augers** take peels and plugs/cores away separately.
