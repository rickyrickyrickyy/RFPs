Introduction

The Enclosure / shell (Subsystem 9) guards operators from moving parts — belt/pulleys, plunger drive, and peeler assemblies — while allowing fast access for cleaning, service, and inspection. It consists of two main covers (plunger-side and drivetrain-side) mounted to the frame using overcentered hinges that hold each lid in a stable open or closed position. Three lid safety interlocks (coded magnet switches) feed into the controls and electronics subsystem to prevent operation when any cover is open.

Colour key & components

Two main covers + overcentered hinges. Feeder, drivetrain lid, and plunger lid all use overcentered hinges (open stay open, shut stay shut).
