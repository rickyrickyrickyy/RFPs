Questions for RFP

Working checklist (moved out of primary site nav). Use per subsystem/subassembly to flush parameters, tolerances, and missing specs. Last updated: 2026-03-20.

1. Fruit intake system

  • What are the full parameters and tolerances for the feeder body, 90° entry tube (with CIP nozzle), and external duct (oranges from hopper)?
  • Top-level feeder: photos and Q&A for components not yet covered by the feeding subassembly?
  • How does the feeder body interface with the enclosure and the extraction/sync fruit support?

Feeding subassembly

  • CIP vertical cut-out for spray nozzle: when will it be added to CAD, and what are the exact dimensions/position?
  • 90° pipe: confirm equivalent China-available standard (3″ NPS / 90 DN Schedule 5S, 88.9 mm OD, 2.11 mm wall) and supplier.

2. Extraction & synchronisation

  • Fruit support: Confirm target 8° rail angle (vs current 3°) and any tolerance. When will this be updated in CAD?
  • Fruit support pusher: Proceed with a formed component instead of cut/welded sheet? Material and form spec?
  • Chute deflectors (WIP): Final geometry and mount details to avoid collision during motion? Material and manufacturability (bent plate)?
  • Parameters and tolerances for driven bracket (yoke mount holes, alignment to static peelers)?
  • Parameters and tolerances for driven peelers and central pin?

Fruit support (subassembly)

  • Indexing slot spacing and tolerance for rail insertion?
  • Connecting link: material and weld/assembly spec?

3. Transmission

  • Yoke: full interface tolerance with cam bearings (drivetrain) and extraction/sync? Alignment to frame?
  • 35 mm drive shafts and LMK35UU bearings: axial location, preload, and mount tolerances?
  • Transmission mount plate: H7/dowel or other alignment to frame?

4. Collection

  • 34 mm standard connector for juice collector Y-tube: exact part number or spec to send to manufacturer?
  • Static deflectors (WIP): Final flat-plate geometry, mount to static loaded plate, and clearance vs chute deflectors? Material and manufacturability?
  • Static peelers: mesh tolerance with driven peelers? Material and finger geometry?
  • Filter tube: slot dimensions and tolerance for juice flow and plunger pass-through?
  • Plug cutter: interface tolerance with core ejection plunger and core chute?

5. Core ejection / juice collection

  • Plunger: stroke, diameter, and tolerance with filter tube and plug cutter?
  • Plunger drive bracket and rods: interface with drivetrain/transmission cycle? 178 mm range confirmation?
  • Juice outflow collection: routing, connections, and cleanability?

6. Drivetrain

  • Sumitomo Cyclo 6000 reducer: Provide full specs (ratio, torque, mounting, shaft dimensions).
  • WEG 22 1450 rpm motor: Provide full specs (power, frame, shaft, protection, terminal box).
  • V-belt (DIN 2215 17×957, 2-belt, 1000 mm): tensioning procedure and replacement interval?
  • Cam bearings: interface tolerance with transmission yoke? Lubrication and service?

7. Disposal system

  • Peel chute and core chutes: material (e.g. folded sheet), weld spec, and interface with collection/core ejection?
  • Augers: speed, torque, and interface with third-party equipment (Jiangxi line)?

8. Frame

  • Loaded mount plate, transmission mount plate, drivetrain mount plate: final H7/dowel positions and assembly sequence?
  • Leveling feet (6111K669 M24): confirm quantity and load rating for JX floor?

9. Enclosure / shell

  • Overcentered hinges: The feeder, drivetrain lid, and plunger lid use overcentered hinges (open position when open, pressure down when shut). Springs and assembly method for these hinges are not designed yet — specify spring type, preload, pivot geometry, and assembly/fastening method.
  • Plunger cover (#BC4944) and drivetrain cover (#489C26): confirm interface with frame and hinge mounting points; any gasketing or sealing when shut?
  • Safety: lid switches (see Controls) and access for CIP/maintenance?

10. Clean-in-place (CIP)

  • Nozzle positions at each peeler and at each 90° tube: flow rate and spray pattern spec?
  • Pipes feeding CIP nozzles: routing, material, and connection to third-party CIP equipment?

11. Controls and electronics

  • Yoke position / cycle: Encoder or other position detection for cycle and controlled stop — specify solution (TBD / needs further development).
  • WEG W22 motor: spec sheet and integration with contactor/starter?
  • Lid safety switches: quantity, position, and interlock logic?

General design and manufacturing

  • Material for every component: confirm list per subsystem.
  • Manufacturing method per part (e.g. formed vs welded for fruit support pusher).
  • Fasteners: Full selection and assembly process per joint.
  • Tolerancing: How will manufacturers achieve critical interfaces (e.g. H7/dowel)? Post-processing (ream, etc.)?
  • CAD: Inventor → SolidWorks transition — what is the timeline and who owns conversion?

Third-party and site integration

  • Jiangxi: confirm interface with Kaiyi or similar orange juicing line (feed in, peels/juice out).
  • Equipment context: JBT Marel–style; assume Chinese equipment interfaces — any specific standards or connectors?

Recommended figures (contractor clarity)

When answering the questions above, tie evidence to subsystem pages. Gaps that commonly need photos or diagrams:

  • Add figure: Per-subsystem "proof pack" — one sheet per major interface (feeder in, juice out, CIP, electrical) with dimensions.
  • Add figure: Alignment / dowel workflow — photos or CAD for frame → plates → peelers chain.
  • Add figure: DFM risk callouts — welds, drains, and washdown dead zones you want the contractor to address.
